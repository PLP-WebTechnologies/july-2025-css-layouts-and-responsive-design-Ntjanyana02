When to use Grid vs. Flexbox

Use Grid when you place items in two dimensions (rows and columns), and Flexbox when aligning items in one direction (row or column) with control over spacing and order.

  • Grid: full-page or section layouts with defined areas.
  • Flex: navigation bars, card rows, toolbars.

Responsive strategies

Combine relative units (%, vw, rem) with media queries at a few purposeful breakpoints. Let content drive the layout rather than fixed device sizes.

  1. Start mobile-first with a single column.
  2. Enhance at medium widths (add a sidebar or second column).
  3. Scale to desktop (wider gutters, multi-column grids).
